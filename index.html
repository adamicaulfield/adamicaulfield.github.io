About Me

I am a student at Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) pursuing a Ph.D. in Computing and Information Sciences as a part of the Global Cybersecurity Institute (GCI). I was accepted to RIT's Ph.D. program in the Fall semester of 2020. My advisor is Dr. Ivan De Oliveira Nunes, and I am a member of the CHAOS-Sec lab.

Before I was accepted to RIT's Ph.D. program, I attended the University of Delaware. At the University of Delaware, I completed a Master of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering (M.S. ECE) in 2020 and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (B.S. CE) in 2019.
