About Me

In 2025, I began working as post-doctoral researcher at University of Waterloo in CrySP within the David R. Cheriton School of Computer Science. In this position, I work under Prof. N. Asokan in the Secure Systems Group (SSG) of CrySP on topics of Platform Security and Security of ML-based Systems

.

I received a Ph.D. from Rochester Institute of Technology in (RIT) 2025 in Computing and Information Sciences. I was advised by Prof. Ivan De Oliveira Nunes and was a member of the CHAOS-Sec lab within the Global Cybersecurity Institute (GCI).

Before I was accepted to RIT's Ph.D. program, I attended the University of Delaware. At the University of Delaware, I completed a Master of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering (M.S. ECE) in 2020 and a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering (B.S. CE) in 2019.
